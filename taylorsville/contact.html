Board of Directors

Board of Directors - 2021

As a non-profit 501c 6 organization, the Chamber is governed by a volunteer based Board of Directors who provide leadership and oversight to the organization. The Board of Directors meet bi-monthly on the even months of the year to review financials, and membership status. Board Members are nominated to serve for a two year term with the ability to serve for two terms or more if elected by the board to serve in a leadership role. The Executive Committee consists of a Board Chair, a Vice Chair, a Past Chair, and a Secretary/Treasurer. Board nominations typically take place starting in July of each year with the election from the general membership completed by the end of September.
