Become a Member

Become a member today!

As a Chamber of Commerce, the goal is to provide and support our local businesses. We believe everyone can prosper with access to our essential resources. By presenting upcoming events, weather forecasts, and business listings, planning for your economic future is what we strive to attain. It’s important for our community to help our local businesses and services. Become a member today and access our business listings to find the services and resources you need.

Business Information
Choose Membership Level

Non-profit organizations will receive a framed certificate of membership and a subscription to our weekly newsletter. You will also receive access to our online membership directory.

Bronze members will also have access to our partnered member discounts and promotional offers. Your event listings can also be published online and in our newsletter.

Silver members will also have local newspaper articles submitted for ribbon cuttings, and business opening events. Marketing opportunities and educational material will also be provided.

Gold members get everything plus invitations to our luncheon events to network with our most prominent community leaders. Gold members will also be promoted to our organization’s Board of Governors with a featured listing that prominently highlights your business and services.
