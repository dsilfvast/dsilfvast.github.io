Description of Target

As a primary focus, our target audience has a need to know the weather. There will always be something in the works, and our visitors will need to know what to anticipate throughout the week. The target might not be fully computer literate, but the site functionality should be simple and clean to provide a pleasant user experience. Our target will most likely reside in or near Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. They’ll have an interest in local events and news reports.

Scenarios

Max is often looking for an adventure. If it's going to snow, he'll be ready! If the sun is shining, he's making plans to cook up a feast! He needs to know what the weather is going to be like in the next few days. This site will provide that information.

Shirayuki is planting a garden this year. Rabbits need to eat, but their food can't handle a hard frost. She'll be relying on local reports to find out when it's safe to plant a garden. With our website providing local news, the information will surely be available.

Is it going to rain today? If so, Max needs to know. He'd hate to have his lawn burn up when his boy can be watering it down with the hose. By providing information about the current weather, our site can help Max deligate his responsibilities.

Volunteer work is hard work! Shirayuki can always find people in need of her assistance with the local news postings. The soup kitchen is often mentioned when help is needed most. With a website focused on three specific locations, there should be ample opportunities to serve the community.

Is that snow rolling in? Max runs a side business with a plow. There's fierce competition out there and our forecast will give ample warning to be prepared.

Illustrators are always in need of inspiration. The gallery within our pages will provide fresh new images of the majestic scenery for Shirayuki to peruse.
